- About Us -

Our company has established since Early 2016 by Michael Alexander and Tony Setiawan, and that's where MTgrahpy came from. MTgraphy first opened its service in Singapore as a small photography service for tourists. At that moment MTgraphy only had 2 employees, Michael and Tony themselves, but from time to time, MTgraphy started to grow bigger and have more than 300 employees in 3 countries in South-East Asia including Singapore, Indonesia, and Malaysia

Now MTgraphy is one of the best photography company in Malaysia, Singapore and Indonesia. We offer our best service of photography in a lot of fields, starting from Pre-Wedding Photography, Year Book Photography, Natural Photography, Product Photography, Commercial Photography, Aesthetic Photography, Architectural Photography, Event Photography, Macro Photography, Social Media Photography, Food Photography and Lifestyle Photography. If you are interested in our services please contact us, we promise to give our best service until our customer is satisfied
